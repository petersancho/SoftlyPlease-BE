Research Project

This platform represents cutting-edge research in computational design, developed by Peter J Wingo-Sancho. The system integrates advanced geometric algorithms with real-time web-based visualization, enabling parametric design exploration through an intuitive interface. Each example demonstrates the power of algorithmic geometry generation, from Delaunay triangulation to complex surface manipulation.
